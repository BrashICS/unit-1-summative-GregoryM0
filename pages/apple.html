Iphone

The Iphone is an incredible product which everyone uses in the modern day, but what was its importance to computer science? The Iphone held a lot of importance in this subject, as it was the first popular device to make it easy to browse desktop websites on a mobile device. The Iphone revolutionized the mobile web experience, introducing things like touchscreens and popularizing the concept of mobile apps, which they did through their very own apps, like the App Store. Overall, Apple changed the world, the way we interact with technology, the way we see tech, the way we converse with others, close or far, and has had heavy impacts on many things through the new communication abilities and advancements that came with it. Each new Iphone brings something new, better equipment, parts, cameras, the innovation on this one product is unbelievable and it is hard to gather all of the things this one product helps us with in just our own daily lives.

Ipod

The Ipod may not have had as many contributions to computer science, but its impacts in the world are clear. The Ipods ability to provide free content like music disrupted whole industries, for instance, music like stated above, the news/newspapers, and movies as well. The Ipod was not as influential in technical terms, but in the importance of the product, it stands side by side with the Iphone. With Steve Jobs at the center of both products, it's still hard to say that the ipod had no implications in computer science, for example itunes as a new app which influenced later apps like spotify or apple music. Even these small things had an effect on the world we live in today, so the importance of the Ipod to Computer Science still stands through the technological advancements which came with it, and the groundwork it led out for future apps.

Mac OS

Again, the Mac OS isn't as groundbreaking as the iphone in its direct impacts on computer science and how it's done, but the Mac OS is still crucial to coding and computer science. This is because it has been used by programmers for a long time, the Mac OS supports tools and languages such as Python, Ruby, Perl, as well as many others like Unix Systems and Html and Css. The Mac OS is based on Unix and easily runs nearly all Linux software, which makes it such an important piece when it comes to computer science and more directly the programming and coding done within it.
